Zambia: Planning a Trip to the Kasanka Bat Migration

2024 · Practical Notes

Every year between late October and mid-December, millions of straw-coloured fruit bats congregate in a small patch of swamp forest in Kasanka National Park, Zambia. It is one of the largest mammal migrations on Earth.

This article collects practical information for anyone considering a similar trip: timing, logistics, costs, and what to expect.
